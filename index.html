Professional

Distribuidora de Carnes "El Faisán"

June 2018 - current

Detailed Product Branch Manager  Adapting to El Faisán was a huge challenge for me. The company was founded on the 70's and the CEO managed it in the same way he did since the beginning. While the numbers stayed decent, there weren't as big as they did in their best years.

When I started working at El Faisán, I saw a lot of opportunity for growth through simple changes in everyday tasks (as any good millenial would), but most of my ideas were recieved with doubt.

So I decided to take the passenger seat for a while and learn more about the industry. Meat industry in Mexico is a very informal business and a lot of the things I learned in college weren't as easy to implement as I thought at the beginning, this helped me understand why a lot of what I percieved as easy solutions weren't well received.

Still, the numbers talked for themselves and there was a lot of room for improvement, so I took a different approach. Instead of focusing on big changes I focused on learning about employees and clients, their thoughts about the company and their everyday problems. With their insights I was able to implement some improvements (mostly digital) and was given the opportunity to create a new product branch.

Some of my achievements at El Faisán include:

- Grew revenue by 30% per kilogram sold over one year by creating a new product line with added value customized for the end consumer.

- Increased sales by 5% QoQ by building an online presence and designing a digital marketing strategy from scratch.

- Contributed to 14% ($2.9M pesos) increase in QoQ sales by identifying customer needs and enabling their satisfaction.
